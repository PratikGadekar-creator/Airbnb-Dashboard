# рџ“Љ Dashboard Insights

### рџЊЌ Market Concentration

Paris, New York City, and Sydney together account for nearly **50% of all Airbnb listings** and approximately **48% of total customer reviews**, making them the platform's most active markets.

---

### рџЏ™пёЏ City Performance

Paris has the highest number of listings as well as customer reviews.

A possible business explanation is that hotel accommodation in Paris is generally much more expensive, making Airbnb a preferred and cost-effective choice for travelers.

---

### в­ђ Customer Satisfaction

Mexico City and Rio de Janeiro achieved the highest overall guest ratings, indicating a consistently positive customer experience.

In contrast, Hong Kong and Istanbul received comparatively lower ratings.

Across most cities, **Cleanliness** and **Value for Money** were the review categories with the lowest average scores, highlighting potential areas for improvement.

---

### рџ‘Ґ Customer Review Behaviour

Customer review activity indicates that most Airbnb users review properties only occasionally.

- **98.85%** of reviewers submitted **three or fewer reviews**.
- Only a very small percentage of customers reviewed multiple properties.

One reviewer submitted **283 reviews**, with the last two reviews posted on **13 October 2020** for two different listings in Bangkok. This unusually high activity may represent either an exceptional frequent traveler or a potential data quality anomaly.

---

### рџ›ЎпёЏ Host Trust & Verification

More than **two-thirds of Airbnb hosts have fully verified profiles**, while nearly every host provides at least one trust signal.

This reflects Airbnb's strong emphasis on establishing trust and transparency between hosts and guests.

---

### рџ“… Seasonal Travel Trends

Customer review activity clearly reflects seasonal tourism patterns.

- Paris and Rome experience their highest review activity between **April and August**, corresponding with the peak European summer travel season.
- New York City shows increased review activity during **November and December**, aligning with holiday travel and year-end tourism.

---

## рџ’Ў Key Takeaways

- Airbnb demand is highly concentrated in a few major tourist destinations.
- Higher hotel prices appear to influence Airbnb adoption in cities like Paris.
- Guest satisfaction varies significantly by city.
- Trust signals and host verification contribute to platform credibility.
- Seasonal travel trends strongly influence booking and review activity.
- Customer review behaviour suggests that most guests are infrequent reviewers.
